## How it works

The API acts as a server-side proxy between your app and video CDNs. When you request a download, the API resolves the direct CDN URL, bypasses CDN authentication requirements, and streams the video bytes directly to your client — without storing any data on disk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fetch video metadata">
    Call `POST /api/metadata` with a video URL to get the title, thumbnail, duration, and platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolve the download URL">
    Call `POST /api/get-direct-url` to get a proxied stream URL ready for download.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the video">
    Use the returned URL with `GET /api/stream` to download the video with real-time `Content-Length` progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>
